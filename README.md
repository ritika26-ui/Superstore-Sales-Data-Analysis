#  Superstore Sales Data Analysis (Python Project)

##  Objective

To analyze sales and profit data across regions, categories, and time to identify key business insights and improve profitability.

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* Matplotlib, Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

##  Key Performance Indicators

* Total Sales: 2.29M+
* Total Orders: 9986
* Profit Margin: Calculated using sales and profit data

---

##  Key Analysis Performed

* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Loss Analysis by Category
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales Trend Over Time
* Correlation Analysis
* Discount vs Profit Analysis

---

##  Key Insights

* Furniture category contributes highest losses
* Higher discounts reduce profitability
* Sales show growth over time
* Revenue is concentrated in top-performing products

---

##  Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategies
* Focus on high-margin products
* Improve loss-making categories
* Use data-driven decision making
